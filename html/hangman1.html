*** HANGMAN! ***


  1. The main idea of this game is to guess the word letter by letter. Player has 6 attempts "lives" to enter a letter and 2 minutes to finish the game.
  2. Enter a letter of the alphabet and click "Check It!" button.
  3. If the letter is contained in the word, it will appear and will be removed from the list of alphabet. After that guess an another letter and enter it.
  4. If the letter is not contained in the word, it won't appear. You can enter an another letter.
  5. The game continues until: the word is guessed (all letters are revealed) – WINNER or, all attempts were used and word isn't guessed – LOSER.
